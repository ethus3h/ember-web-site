How the collection is structured

It is not complete yet, but eventually this is what should be available:

  • Releases
    • Links to track pages (as found through "Works")
    • Album artwork
    • Metadata
    • Copies of (through the Internet Archive):
      • Audio:
        • Lossless audio for all-digital releases
        • Lossless CD rips, except for copyright-encumbered compilations (available tracks will be released)
        • Manufacturing process files for CD releases, except for copyright-encumbered compilations (if any)
        • CD images for CD releases, except for copyright-encumbered compilations
        • Original release files for digital releases, where available
        • ZIP releases for 2009–2010
        • Production files for digitally-produced single-track releases
        • Recorded audio of analog-media releases
        • Release production files of digitally-produced releases
      • Artwork:
        • Lossless album artwork for digital releases
        • Archival quality photographs or scans of album artwork for analog media and CDs
        • Artwork production files
        • Archival quality photographs or scans of analog media
      • Video:
        • Video content of video releases
        • Video production files where available
  • Works
    • Links to releases on which individual works are found
    • Other versions of tracks
    • Production files for individual tracks when not associated with specific releases
    • Scores and scorewriter files
    • Metadata
  • Broadcasts
    • Broadcast recordings where available and non-copyright-encumbered
  • Live performances
    • Live recordings where available and non-copyright-encumbered
  • Press mentions
    • Press references where available and non-copyright-encumbered

How the switched é/e in Anoé text is handled: All text is in such a manner that it displays as it does in the album artwork, even if that is not correct Anoé. This typographical issue is not corrected from album artwork to transcription here, rather the original is left intact. Note that the Internet Archive item identifier for "Itfaen-atél-eselit" (itfaén-atel-ésélit properly) does correct the error as of 1 September 2013, although that may be changed.
