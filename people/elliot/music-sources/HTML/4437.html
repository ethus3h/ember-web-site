 Description:

A list of characters in various scripts. 17334159 characters in 258 categories are currently listed (though some may be incomplete/have typos/be duplicates/have some other dorky error). It is derived from what would have been version 1.9 (or maybe 2.0, depending on what I decided) of the Character Encoding (derived from many standards and lists, most notably the Unicode standard), which has been abandoned (but may be resurrected in the future). If you find mistakes or want new character(s) listed, please e-mail me (please do so!).
