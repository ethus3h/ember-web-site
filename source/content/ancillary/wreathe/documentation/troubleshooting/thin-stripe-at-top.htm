Page location: Software Wreathe Documentation Troubleshooting

This article applies to: Wreathe 7.2

Problem

There is a thin (~1px) stripe of color at the top of the screen (can appear to be that the Plasma desktop background / wallpaper is shifted down a pixel)

(See this (resolved) bug)

Solution

You have gnome-panel running. That is an automatically-hidden gnome-panel.
