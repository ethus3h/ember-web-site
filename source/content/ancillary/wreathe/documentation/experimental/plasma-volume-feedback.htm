Plasma volume widget plays audio feedback on volume change even when configured not to — Wreathe Documentation

Page location: Software Wreathe Documentation Troubleshooting

This article applies to: Wreathe 7.3

Problem

The Plasma volume widget plays audio feedback on volume change even when configured not to.

Solution (workaround)

Rebooting seems to make the play-volume-change-feedback option of the Plasma volume widget take effect.
