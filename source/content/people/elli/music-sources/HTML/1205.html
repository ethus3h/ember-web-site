Руки Вверх!.

Руки Вверх! is a Russian pop group.

The name, 'Руки Вверх!', means 'Hands Up!', and can be transliterated as 'Ruki Vverh!' (the conventional transliteration, however, is 'Ruki Vverkh').

It is, on several album covers (Маленькие девочки, Не бойся, я с тобой!, Конец попсе, танцуют все, Мне с тобою хорошо), typeset as ''.

It was active until 2006. If there are any pieces missing from this list, or you have more information pertaining to the group, please e-mail it to me.

The albums are sorted chronologically.
