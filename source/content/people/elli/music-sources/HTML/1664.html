Antibiotic-Resistant Bacteria

Due to the lack of sufficient space in factory farms, the livestock develop diseases easily, warranting vast quantities of antibiotics for the their control. “This overuse of antibiotics creates super-strains of disease-resistant germs”[2]. These bacteria then pose significant problems for hospitals if they infect the patients' wounds, as the bacteria's resistance renders antibiotics ineffective.
