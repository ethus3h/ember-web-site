Libera.

Libera is a UK boys' choir.

It has previously been known as 'St. Philips Choir', 'The St Philips Boy's Choir', and 'Angel Voices'.

If there are any pieces missing from this list, you have more information pertaining to the group, or there are mistakes in this list, please e-mail it to me.
