====== How to play "Flash" Internet content embedded in WWW pages ======

To enable "Flash" playback:

  * Open the Internet WWW viewer
  * Click "Tools" in the top toolbar
  * Hover over "Flash-Aid"
  * Hover over "Quick Mode"
  * Click "Install beta flash"
  * Click "OK"
  * Enter your password when prompted (if you haven't [[change the default password|changed it]], it is by default ''elen-esar'')
  * Press 'Enter'
  * Follow the instructions presented in the window into which you typed your password
