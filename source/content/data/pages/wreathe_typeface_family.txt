**Wreathe** is a serifed typeface family begun ~2011. It consists of the following typefaces:

  * [[Wreathe (typeface)|Wreathe]] | A Roman typeface
  * [[Wreathe Italic]] | An Italic typeface
  * [[WreatheBoldItal]] | A bold Italic typeface
  * [[WreatheM]] | A variant Roman typeface
  * [[WreatheSC]] | A small capital / old-style numeral Roman typeface
  * [[Wreathe Unicode]] | A pan-Unicode typeface
  * [[Wreathe Han A JP]] | A Han Unicode typeface
  * [[Wreathe Han B JP]] | A Han Unicode typeface
