a.

 Definitions:

  1. Adjective. Used to refer to any object or person not yet mentioned: This is a sentence. -or- A woman entered. This form is used prior to consonants; use an prior to vowels and the aspirate. When referring to an object or person previously mentioned, use the.

 Synonyms:

  1. [1] an
  2. [1] the indefinite article

 Etymology:

  1. [1] weak form of Old English ān
