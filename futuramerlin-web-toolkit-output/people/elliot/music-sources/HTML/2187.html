Descriptors of French letters:

Large letter: majuscule | example: A: A majuscule | used on letters: (any)
Small letter: minuscule | example: a: a minuscule | used on letters: (any)
repeated letter: deux | example: ll: deux l used on letters: (any that repeat)
´ (acute accent): l'accent aigu | example: é: e accent aigu | used on letter: é
` (grave accent): l'accent grave | example: à: accent grave | used on letters: è, à, ò
ˆ (circumflex accent): l'accent circonflexe | example: â: a accent circonflexe | used on letters: â, ê, î, ô, û
¸ (cedilla): la cédille | example: ç: c cédille | used on letter: ç
¨ (dieresis): le tréma | example: ë: e trema | used on letters: ë, ï
