Plasma panel is grey but Compiz is running — Wreathe Documentation

Page location: Software Wreathe Documentation Troubleshooting

Table of Contents

  1. Problem
  2. Solution (workaround)

This article applies to: Wreathe 7.2

Problem

Plasma panel sometimes appears grey instead of transparent, even when Compiz is running (see bug)

Solution (workaround)

Clicking on the panel (or on grey bits of it?) fixes either some or all of the grey part(s). Click more than once as needed. It can be a little hard to click right on the grey parts when the grey part is small (e. g. when it's around the quicklaunch icons, IIRC); just try to hit it again.
