How to play Adobe Flash Internet content embedded in WWW pages — Wreathe Documentation

Page location: Software Wreathe Documentation How-To

This article applies to: Wreathe 7.2, possibly Wreathe 5

Using Adobe Flash, or any other non-libre software, is no longer recommended.

To enable "Flash" playback:

  1. Open the Internet WWW viewer
  2. Click "Tools" in the top toolbar
  3. Hover over "Flash-Aid"
  4. Hover over "Quick Mode"
  5. Click "Install beta flash"
  6. Click "OK"
  7. Enter your password when prompted (if you haven't changed it, it is by default elen-esar)
  8. Press 'Enter'
  9. Follow the instructions presented in the window into which you typed your password
