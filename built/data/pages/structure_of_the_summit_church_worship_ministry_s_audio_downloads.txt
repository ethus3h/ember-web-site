**Structure of The Summit Church Worship Ministry's audio downloads**

For each audio file, there are at least three URLs that should be downloaded.

The first two are simple, following these two patterns, where [file] is the filename of the audio.

  * <code>http://streaming14.finalweb.net/19/140002285/140002285/audio/[file]</code>
  * <code>http://www.summitworshipteam.com/home/140002285/140002285/audio/[file]</code>

The next is more complicated. It is needs an alteration of characters in the filename. At least the following replacements need to be made:

  * space → +
  * . → %252E

The pattern is as follows, where [file-altered] is the filename of the audio with the above mappings applied.

  http://www.summitworshipteam.com/ministryinc/audiodownloads2.asp?flashVars=playerID%3D1%26amp%3BsoundFile%3Dhttp%3A%2F%2Fwww.summitworshipteam.com%2Fhome%2F140002285%2F140002285%2Faudio%2F[file-altered]
